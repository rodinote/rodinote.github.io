1. Data We Collect

Stored on your device: App preferences, theme, notification settings, trash bin contents. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Account information: When signing in with Apple or Google; your email (Apple's hide-email is supported), display name, and unique ID are processed by Firebase Authentication.

Content processed for AI: When you use the AI card generator; your text, PDF, or photo input is sent through our server proxy to Google Gemini API. This content is not stored — only held in memory temporarily during processing. Per Google's official policy, it is not used for AI training.

Advertising data (free users only): Google AdMob collects advertising ID (IDFA/GAID), device type, approximate location (IP-based), and interaction data to serve ads. iOS shows the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt — you can decline. Premium and Max users see no ads, and this data is not collected.

Subscription data: RevenueCat processes anonymous user ID, purchase history, and platform info. Payment details (card numbers, etc.) are processed only by Apple App Store or Google Play; we never see them.
